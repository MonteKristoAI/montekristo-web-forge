How Leads Come In — 5 Channels

Every channel below automatically creates or updates a contact in GoHighLevel. Chris and Campbell never need to manually enter a lead from any of these sources.

Website Form

Quote Request Form

When someone fills out the quote form on the LuxeShutters website, their details land instantly in GHL as a new contact tagged website-quote-request. The contact is created with their name, phone, email, address, and the product type they selected.

Captures: First name, last name, phone, email, address, city, service type, urgency/timeframe

Website Form

Consultation Request Form

When someone books a consultation through the website, their contact is created in GHL tagged website-consultation-request. Their message or special request is saved directly to the contact's Notes field.

Captures: Full name, phone, email, message/notes

AI Agent

AI Web Chat Widget

The chat widget on the website is powered by Retell AI. It has a full conversation with the visitor, qualifies them, collects their details, and when the chat ends, a summary is sent to GHL. A new opportunity is created in the pipeline at "New Enquiry" stage automatically.

Captures: Name, phone, email, product type, enquiry type, timeframe, suburb, postcode, property type, window count, and a full chat summary in the Notes field.

AI Agent

AI Phone Voice Agent

When someone calls the LuxeShutters number, the AI voice agent answers, qualifies the caller, and collects all their details. After the call ends, the full call analysis is sent to GHL. A contact and opportunity are created automatically — including the call transcript summary in Notes.

Captures: Name, phone, email, product interest, enquiry type, timeframe, suburb, window count, and AI-generated call summary

Website

Negative Feedback Form (Review Gating)

The website review section routes customers based on their star rating. Customers who give 4–5 stars are sent directly to your Google Review page. Customers who give 1–3 stars are shown a private feedback form. That feedback is captured in GHL as a task assigned to Chris, so nothing slips through without being addressed. The customer never posts publicly — you get the chance to resolve it first.

Captures: Star rating, categories of complaint, improvement suggestion, message — all saved as a GHL task for follow-up

Section 5

Quote Generator — Deep Dive

The Quote Generator is the most powerful workflow in the system. It reads all the job details from GHL, calculates the price automatically (where possible), builds a formatted quote in Xero with per-window line items, and links it back to the GHL card.

Luxe — Quote Generator

Triggered when an opportunity is moved to "Quote Requested" stage in GHL

Auto-triggers on stage change
  • 1
    Receive the trigger GHL sends a webhook when the stage changes to "Quote Requested", containing the opportunity ID and contact ID.
  • 2
    Fetch opportunity and contact details The workflow retrieves the full opportunity from GHL — all custom fields including product type, measurements, mount config, colour, notes, markup %, and any manually-entered cost price.
  • 3
    Auto-calculate cost price For Shutters and Roller Blinds, the system calculates the ex-GST cost price from embedded pricing tables (sourced from the CW Global supplier price list). For other products, Chris enters the cost price manually on the GHL card first. If Chris has entered a cost price override, that always takes priority.
  • 4
    Refresh Xero access token The system securely retrieves and refreshes the Xero OAuth token before making any Xero API calls.
  • 5
    Build and create Xero draft quote A draft quote is created in Xero with individual line items — one per window, showing the exact dimensions (e.g. "Shutters — 1200mm × 2100mm (Inside Mount) — 03 White"). The consumer price is cost × (1 + markup %) with the default markup set at 40%.
  • 6
    Save quote details back to GHL The Xero Quote ID, quote URL (direct link to view), cost price, and consumer price are all written back to the opportunity card in GHL so Chris can see everything in one place.
  • 7
    Move deal to "Quote Sent" The deal card is automatically advanced in the pipeline, which in turn starts the follow-up sequence.

Automatic Pricing — How It Works

✅ Shutters (Oasis) — Auto-priced

Price is calculated using a simple area formula based on the CW Global Oasis price list. Each window is calculated separately and summed.

$202 per m² · minimum 1m² per window

Example: A window 1200mm × 2100mm = 2.52m² → $509.04 cost price

✅ Roller Blinds (Atlas Cat 1) — Auto-priced

Uses the exact pricing table from the CW Global Roller Blinds price sheet. Width and height are looked up in the table to return the precise ex-GST cost.

2D lookup table — 24 widths × 14 heights

Example: 1200mm × 2100mm → $102 cost price

⚙️ Curtains — Manual entry

Chris enters the cost price on the GHL opportunity card before moving to "Quote Requested". The system uses that value directly.

⚙️ Awnings, Zipscreens, Security Shutters — Manual

Same as Curtains — Chris enters cost price manually. The system then uses it to calculate consumer price and build the Xero quote.

💡

Override is always possible

If Chris enters a cost price in the GHL card, it always overrides the auto-calculated price — even for Shutters and Roller Blinds. This is useful when there's a special discount, a supplier price change, or an unusual configuration that doesn't fit the standard table.

Section 6

Quote Follow-up — 11-Day Sequence

Once a quote is sent, the system automatically follows up with the customer over 11 days. Each step checks that the deal hasn't already been won or moved — if it has, the remaining follow-ups are cancelled automatically.

Luxe — Quote Follow-up

Triggered automatically when a deal moves to "Quote Sent" stage

11-day sequence
  • 1
    Day 0 — Quote created & deal moved to Quote Sent Chris reviews the Xero draft quote, makes any adjustments, and sends it to the customer. The follow-up timer starts from this point.
  • 2
    Day 3 — Friendly follow-up email An automated email is sent to the customer: "Hi [Name], we wanted to follow up on your LuxeShutters quote. If you have any questions, we'd love to hear from you." The email includes a direct link to view the quote in Xero. If the deal has already moved to Won or another stage, this step is skipped.
  • 3
    Day 6 — SMS reminder A short, friendly SMS is sent: "Hi [Name], just a quick reminder from LuxeShutters about your quote. Happy to chat through any questions — call us on 1800 465 893 or just reply." Again, skipped if the deal has moved on.
  • 4
    Day 11 — Personal call task for Chris A task is created in GHL assigned to Chris: "CALL: Quote no response — 11 days. Client has not responded after 2 follow-up touchpoints. Make a personal call." The contact is also tagged quote-no-response for easy filtering.

Sale Won — What Happens Automatically

This is where the most time is saved. Moving a deal to "Won" triggers a cascade of actions that previously would have taken 30–60 minutes of manual work: raising an invoice in Xero, placing the production order with the supplier, and creating the install task.

Luxe — Sale Won

Triggered when an opportunity is moved to "Won" stage in GHL

Instant — runs in seconds
  • 1
    Fetch opportunity and contact from GHL The workflow reads all job details, pricing, and customer information from the GHL opportunity card.
  • 2
    Create Xero invoice A final invoice is created in Xero using the same line items and consumer pricing as the quote. The invoice is set to AUTHORISED status. The Invoice ID and direct URL are saved back to the GHL opportunity card.
  • 3
    Submit production order to Cora CW Trade HUB An order is submitted to the supplier (Cora) via their API. The order includes each window as a separate line item with the correct dimensions and product configuration. Supported products: Shutters (Oasis), Roller Blinds (Atlas), Curtains, and Awnings. The Cora Order ID is saved to the GHL card for reference.
  • 4
    Create installation task in GHL A task is created: "Schedule installation appointment — [customer name]" with a due date of 24 hours from now. This reminds Chris to contact the customer and lock in the install date.
  • 5
    Move deal to "Invoice Sent" stage The pipeline card is automatically advanced so the stage always reflects the true status of the deal.

Two AI agents are deployed on Retell AI: one answers phone calls and one handles web chats. Both agents are connected to the automation system — when a conversation ends, the data flows straight into GHL.

Answers inbound calls 24/7

The phone agent is a natural-sounding AI voice agent that answers calls to the LuxeShutters number. It introduces itself as a LuxeShutters representative, asks the caller what they're looking for, collects their contact details, and qualifies their enquiry.

When the call ends, Retell AI analyses the call and sends a structured summary to n8n, which creates a contact and opportunity in GHL automatically. The full call summary is saved to the Notes field.

💬 AI Web Chat Widget

Qualifies leads on the website 24/7

The chat widget sits on the LuxeShutters website. When a visitor opens it, the AI starts a conversation — asking about what products they're interested in, their timeline, property type, how many windows, and capturing their name and contact details.

When the chat ends, a summary and all collected data flow into n8n and then into GHL — a contact is created or updated, and an opportunity is opened at "New Enquiry" stage.

Section 9

Integrations — Xero & Cora

Two external systems are connected to the automation: Xero for accounting (quotes and invoices) and Cora CW Trade HUB for supplier production orders.

Xero — Accounting

The automation is connected to LuxeShutters' Xero account via OAuth. It creates quotes and invoices programmatically using the Xero API. Key things to know:

  • Quotes are created as DRAFT — Chris reviews and edits before sending
  • Invoices are created as AUTHORISED — ready for Chris to send to customer
  • Each window is a separate line item on the quote/invoice
  • Prices are exclusive of GST — Xero adds GST at 10% automatically
  • Quote and invoice URLs are saved back to GHL for easy access
⚠️

One-time setup required

Chris needs to visit one URL once to authorise the Xero connection. After that, the system handles token refresh automatically forever.

Cora CW Trade HUB — Supplier Orders

When a sale is won, the system automatically submits a production order to Cora via their trade API. This replaces the manual process of logging into the Cora portal and entering each window individually.

  • Orders submitted for: Shutters, Roller Blinds, Curtains, Awnings
  • Each window becomes a separate order line with its dimensions
  • Cora Order ID is saved back to the GHL opportunity card
  • Chris can look up the order status in the Cora portal using this ID
  • Zipscreens and Security Shutters: Chris orders manually (not in Cora API)
ℹ️

Cora has no pricing API

Cora's API is for placing orders only — it doesn't return pricing. That's why the pricing tables for Shutters and Roller Blinds are embedded directly in the automation.
