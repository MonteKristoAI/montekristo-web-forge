7 Performance Analysis

📈 Organic Traffic — New Performance Tier

The reig-us.com domain entered a structurally different performance tier in late 2025. From September through November 2025, monthly organic traffic held flat at 21 visits — a typical level for a domain that has not yet published targeted content. The launch of the content program in February 2026 preceded a sustained jump to the 83–103 range across the full Q1 2026 period.

The Q1 2026 monthly average of 93 visits represents a 343% improvement over that baseline. For a B2B domain in a niche technical market like solar SCADA and DAS integration, this is meaningful: the visitors arriving through organic search are engineers and project managers with specific procurement intent, not general audiences. A 93-visit average in this vertical is qualitatively different from the same number in a consumer market.

This is not a temporary spike. The traffic has held in the 83–103 range for four consecutive months, confirming that Google has recognized and settled the domain's new authority level.

🔗 Backlinks & Referring Domains — The Authority Engine

Backlinks are the most durable and credible signal Google uses to rank pages. In April 2025, reig-us.com had approximately 305 backlinks from 90 referring domains. By March 2026, those numbers reached 551 backlinks from 159 referring domains — increases of 81% and 77% respectively in under twelve months.

More significantly, March 2026 added 153 new backlinks in a single month — more than the total growth seen in any prior three-month period. This acceleration indicates that as the content library grows, the compound effect of more pages attracting more links is beginning to take hold. Referring domains also hit a new all-time high in March (159), meaning the domain is reaching new audiences and citation sources every week.

These are organic links, earned through published technical content — not purchased or manufactured. They are permanent, and their value to Google's algorithm increases over time as the linking pages themselves gain authority.

📄 Content Strategy — Quality Over Quantity

Publishing launched on February 4, 2026. In the first eight weeks, 16 technical articles were published across six topic clusters: Solar SCADA, DAS, SCADA Integration Services, Fiber Optic Installation, Pyranometer, and Utility-Scale Solar Monitoring. Each article covers a distinct, high-specificity keyword that a qualified buyer would search during a procurement or project planning process.

Every single article passed all 16 Yoast SEO checks — including keyphrase placement, density, meta description quality, internal linking, image optimization, and word count. This standard of execution is not common. It means each article is positioned to rank as high as the domain's current authority permits, with no on-page factor holding it back.

The consistent 2,000+ word count per article (the Pyranometer Installation post contains 2,122 words, and most posts are in this range) signals depth of coverage to Google, which is a direct ranking factor for technical and informational queries.

🤖 AI Visibility — Ahead of the Market

The AI Visibility score of 19, with 10 pages cited across ChatGPT, Google AI Overview, Gemini, and AI Mode, places reig-us.com in a strong position for the emerging AI search landscape. ChatGPT alone cites 7 different pages on the site, suggesting the content is being recognized as a credible technical reference by AI training and retrieval systems.

In B2B procurement, AI-assisted research is becoming standard. Engineers increasingly use ChatGPT and Perplexity to evaluate technical solutions before reaching a vendor's website. A domain that is cited by these systems receives implicit endorsement — its content appears in AI-generated answers as a trusted source, driving awareness and traffic that does not show up in traditional organic traffic metrics.

Competitors in the solar SCADA space who have not yet built an AI-citation-ready content library will find this gap difficult to close quickly, as AI citation is earned through the same long-term content authority signals as traditional SEO.

🔍 Keyword Pipeline — Page 2 to Page 1

The domain currently ranks for 26 keywords. The most significant development is the appearance of high-value technical terms — "solar scada" (position 49) and "scada utility" (positions 34–45) — in Google's index. 🚀 Q2 2026 Outlook

Q2 2026 is positioned to be a breakout period for organic visibility. The March backlink acceleration — +38% in a single month — historically precedes a 60–90 day lag in organic traffic growth, as Google re-crawls and re-weights the pages linking to the domain. Traffic gains from March's backlink surge are expected to materialize in May–June 2026.

The 14 articles published in Q1 are still accumulating their full authority potential. SEO industry data consistently shows that new content takes 3–6 months to reach its peak ranking position. Articles published in February and March will continue to climb through Q2, pulling additional keywords into the 1–20 range and increasing organic traffic further.

If the current publishing cadence of 6–8 technical articles per month continues into Q2, the compounding effect of a growing content library and rising domain authority will accelerate all core metrics: organic traffic, keyword rankings, backlinks, and AI citations.
